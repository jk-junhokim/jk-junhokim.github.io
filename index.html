About Research Blog

I am an undergraduate student at Rice University, majoring in Computer Science. I am currently advised by Prof. Ashok Veeraraghavan and Salman S. Khan. My research interests include lensless imaging, face recognition, and image reconstruction. Recently, I have been transitioning into robot perception and learning, leveraging my background in computer vision and experience from a robotics research internship advised by Prof. Jeannette Bohg. My research focuses on leveraging visual sensory data to scale data for intelligent physical systems. I aim to develop robots that can deeply understand and interact with their physical environments, enhancing their adaptability and autonomy.

I enjoy collaborating and interacting with diverse people. Feel free to reach out!
